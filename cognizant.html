Cognizant — Company Info & Interview Prep

Company History

Cognizant Technology Solutions (CTS) was founded in 1994 as a technology unit of Dun & Bradstreet and became independent later. Headquartered in Teaneck, New Jersey, USA, Cognizant is now a Fortune 500 company that provides IT consulting, digital, and business process services worldwide.

What Cognizant Looks For

  • Good programming and problem-solving skills.
  • Knowledge of web technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript).
  • Database, OS, and cloud fundamentals.
  • Excellent communication and teamwork skills.

Interview Questions

HTML

1. Difference between inline and block elements?
Inline elements do not start on a new line (e.g., <span>), while block elements occupy the full width and start on a new line (e.g., <div>).

CSS

2. Difference between relative and absolute positioning?
Relative is positioned relative to itself, while absolute is positioned relative to the nearest positioned ancestor.

JavaScript

3. Difference between var, let, and const?
Var is function-scoped, let and const are block-scoped. Const cannot be reassigned.

HR Round Questions

1. Tell me about yourself.
Introduce your education, technical skills, and strengths in 60–90 seconds.
2. Why do you want to join Cognizant?
Show interest in digital transformation, innovation, and global career opportunities at Cognizant.

Verbal Communication Round

1. How would you explain your project to a non-technical person?
Avoid jargon, use simple words and analogies, focus on benefits instead of technical complexity.
2. Can you describe a situation where communication solved a conflict?
Talk about listening actively, clarifying doubts, and reaching a mutual solution professionally.
